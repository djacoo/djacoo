## About Me

I'm a graduate student in AI and Computer Science at the University of Verona, focused on building intelligent systems at the intersection of **machine learning**, **reinforcement learning**, and **computational bioinformatics**.

**Currently working on:** Model-based RL, multi-agent systems, and AI applications for scientific discovery.
